Each year the ARCHER2 Celebration of Science brings together the user community to celebrate their scientific achievements on ARCHER2, the UK’s National High Performance Computing Service, which is operated by EPCC.  


Last month we were delighted to welcome over 120 attendees from academia and industry to Edinburgh for the third annual event, alongside the Day 0 workshop.
