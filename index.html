ARCHER2 is the UK National Supercomputing Service

The ARCHER2 Service is a world class advanced computing resource for UK researchers. ARCHER2 is provided by UKRI, EPCC, HPE Cray and the University of Edinburgh.

ARCHER2 is due to commence operation in 2021, replacing the current service ARCHER. For more information on ARCHER, please visit the ARCHER web site.
